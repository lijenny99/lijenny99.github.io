Summary

At Hack the Valley 2018, a Major League Hackathon, my team built a mobile learning platform where students and teachers can create events for tutoring purposes. The project was prototyped in Figma, then built with HTML/CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap on the front end and Firebase as the database. The completed product can be viewed here.
